About Maweza

Delivering excellence in water purification and ice production since 2018

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Mrs. Kelebogile Merriam Moalahi in Kuruman, Northern Cape, Maweza Business Enterprise is a 100% black-owned small business specializing in water purification and ice production.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality purified water and ice products to our community, emphasizing exceptional service, customer satisfaction, and personalized attention.

Our Vision

To expand our operations and become the leading provider of purified water and ice solutions in the Northern Cape region through innovation and community engagement.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your water and ice needs

Water Purification

We purify and bottle water for public consumption, ensuring the highest quality standards and safe drinking water for our customers.

Ice Production

We produce high-quality ice in various quantities to meet diverse needs, from individual consumers to commercial establishments.

Product Distribution

Our products are distributed through a growing network of retail partners across multiple communities in the Northern Cape.

Custom Solutions

We offer tailored solutions for businesses and organizations requiring specialized water purification or ice production services.

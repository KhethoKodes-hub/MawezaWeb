About Maweza Business Enterprise

Your trusted partner for water purification and ice solutions in Kuruman and the Northern Cape

Our Story

Our Beginning

Maweza Business Enterprise was founded in 2018 by Mrs. Kelebogile Merriam Moalahi in Kuruman, Northern Cape. Starting as a small, home-based operation, we have grown into a respected provider of purified water and ice products in the region.

Our Growth

From our humble beginnings operating from a garage, we have expanded our reach across multiple communities. Our reputation for quality and reliability has allowed us to build a strong customer base that continues to grow through word-of-mouth recommendations.

Our Future

We are currently seeking funding to acquire a dedicated building for our operations. This expansion will allow us to consolidate our equipment, streamline processes, and enhance our service delivery to better serve our community.

Our Founder

Founder Image

Mrs. Kelebogile Merriam Moalahi

Founder & Owner

Mrs. Moalahi established Maweza Business Enterprise with a vision to provide high-quality purified water and ice to her community. As a 100% black-owned small business, she has built the company through dedication, personalized customer service, and a commitment to excellence.

Under her leadership, Maweza has become known for its reliable products and community-focused approach, serving customers across Kuruman and surrounding areas.

Our Capacity

Business Structure

Ownership

Maweza Business Enterprise is a 100% black-owned small business registered as a Pty Ltd company. We take pride in our status as a locally-owned enterprise contributing to economic development in our community.

Operations

Currently operating from a garage facility, we have maximized our efficiency within this space. Our operations include water purification, bottling, ice production, and distribution to our growing network of retail partners.

Market Reach

We serve a broad client base spanning from Mothibistad, Magojaneng Mapoteng, Seven Miles, and extending to Batharos. Our reputation for quality continues to expand our reach throughout the Northern Cape region.
